About

Hi, I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science in Turing Class, CKC Honors College, Zhejiang University. During my undergraduate, I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen on generative visual models, Prof. Jake Zhao on data-centric AI and Prof. Yingcai Wu on visual analytics. Currently I am also a visiting research student at Stanford University, supervised by Prof. Jiajun Wu.

My research interests are computer vision and machine learning. My ultimate goal is enabling machines to understand and generate an interactive 3D world. Previously, I have also done engaging projects on VR and HCI.
